LEADERSHIP TEAM

Dr. Grace Nkechinyere Ijoma

Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Ijoma is an internationally recognized expert in wastewater treatment and alternative energy technologies with over 15 years of industrial experience. As CEO, she brings world-class technical expertise and strategic vision to NuCorp Energy.

Her credentials include:

  • Senior Researcher at the University of South Africa (UNISA)
  • Post-doctoral research supervision in Chemical Engineering
  • Lead Researcher at the Institute for the Development of Energy for African Sustainability (IDEAS)
  • Published expert on biogas, algal optimization, microalgae biodiesel, renewable, and anaerobic digestion systems
  • International consultant on African energy initiatives across Kenya, Nigeria, Zambia, Namibia, and Rwanda
  • Designer and implementer of biogas digesters for South African schools and farms
  • Founder of BioSolve SA (Pty) Ltd

SUSTAINABILITY IMPACT

Environmental Benefits

  • GHG Reduction: Each MW of biogas capacity reduces >15,000 tonnes CO2e annually
  • Water Diversion: Preventing methane emissions from landfills and lagoons
  • Water Quality: Significantly improved effluent quality from treatment facilities
  • Water Security: Enhanced water treatment capacity and reuse potential
  • Resource Efficiency: Nutrients and energy from wastewater streams
  • Soil Health: Organic fertilizer replacing chemical inputs

Social Benefits

  • Job Creation: 10-15 direct jobs per MW installed capacity
  • Skills Development: Technical training in wastewater treatment and renewable energy
  • Public Health: Improved sanitation through better sewage treatment
  • Energy Access: Reliable power for communities and businesses
  • Health Improvements: Reduced pollution, improved sanitation and cleaner water

Economic Benefits

  • Energy Security: Reduced reliance on grid and fossil fuels
  • Cost Savings: 30-50% reduction in energy costs, reduced water treatment costs
  • Revenue Generation: New income streams from waste processing and energy sales
  • Infrastructure Value: Modernized wastewater treatment infrastructure
  • Local Development: Stimulating rural and peri-urban economies
